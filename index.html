Subscribe to changes in NYC Restaurant Grades

  1. Login with your Google account. This site doesn't store your account password, that is managed by Google.
  2. Subscribe to your favorite restaurants.
  3. Get an email whenever a restaurant's health grade or score changes. Emails arrive around 8pm EST on the day of the change. Click here to see a sample mail.
  4. Come back to this site at any time to change your subscriptions or delete your account.
